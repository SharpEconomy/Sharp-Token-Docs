FAQs?
Here are some frequently asked questions (FAQs)
1. How do I delete my wallet?
Yes, you can delete your wallet. Go to
Settings -> Remove Wallet
and then a confirmation popup will appear. After that, you need to enter your wallet passcode and click on 'Delete my wallet.' You will then be redirected to the Wallet welcome screen.
2. How can you secure your wallet to set up Face ID?
Yes, you can set up Face ID security. To do so, navigate to Security -> Face ID. After setting up your Face ID, you will be able to unlock your wallet either via Face ID or by entering your wallet passcode.
3. How do I earn tokens?
- You earn tokens by walking 1000 steps, with 1 token awarded for every 1000 steps. The maximum you can earn per day is 10 tokens. You can spin the wheel to win up to 100 Sharp daily. You can refer a friend, you and your friends gets 100 Sharp.
4. What happens if I walk more than 10,000 steps in a day?
- You can still walk more than 10,000 steps, but the maximum number of tokens you can earn per day is 10.
5. Do I need to open the app every day to convert my steps to tokens?
- Yes, you need to open the app at least once daily to convert your steps to tokens.
6. Can I carry over unclaimed steps from one day to the next?
- No, unclaimed steps from previous days will not be considered the next day.
7. Where can I see my steps, calories burnt, and tokens earned today?
- You can see this information on the health screen of the app.
8. How do I check my previous days' health data?
- You can find your previous days' health data under "Steps Today" on the health screen.
9. What is the difference between allocated tokens and available tokens?
- Allocated tokens are temporarily locked and will gradually become unlocked over time. Available tokens are accessible within your wallet for transactions and exchanges.
10. How do I check my reward summary?
- You can check your reward summary under "Recent Transactions" on the wallet screen.
11. Where can I find my wallet address and passphrase?
- Your wallet address is under "address" in the profile screen, and your passphrase can be found in the "Security" section of the profile screen.
12. Can I change the theme of the app?
- Yes, you can choose between dark, light, and default themes from the "Security" section of the profile screen.
13. How can I update my username and profile picture?
- You can update your username and profile picture under the profile screen of the app.
14. Can I activate/deactivate biometric authentication?
- Yes, you can activate/deactivate biometric authentication from the "Security" section of the app.
Thank you for using the Sharp Rewards app!
