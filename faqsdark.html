FAQs?
Here are some frequently asked questions (FAQs)
1. I forgot my passphrase. Can I recover it?
- No, the passphrase is stored only on your device. We do not store any user's passphrase, so it cannot be recovered if it's lost.
2. Is there a daily limit on the number of referrals?
- No, there is no daily limit on referrals.
3. Why can’t I transfer my tokens?
- Token transfers are only allowed to our registered partners. Registered partners are companies that wish to integrate our token into their platform. For any kind of bussiness proposals please reachout to contact@sharpeconomy.org
4. How can I withdraw/redeem Sharp tokens?
- You cannot withdraw or redeem Sharp tokens until Sharp token is listed on a Tier 1 exchange, which is expected to happen by the end of this year.
5. I referred my friend, but didn’t receive 100 tokens. Why?
- This could happen due to the following reasons:

• Your referred friend did not create a wallet after clicking on the referral link.
• There was an internet connectivity issue while your friend was creating their wallet.
• You have reached the maximum referral limit of 100 referrals per Sharp account.
6. Why is my friend unable to create a Sharp wallet?
- A user can create only one Sharp wallet per device. To create a new Sharp wallet, they must use a different device.
7. How do I delete my wallet?
Yes, you can delete your wallet. Go to
Settings -> Remove Wallet
, and a confirmation popup will appear. After that, you need to enter your wallet passcode and click on 'Delete my wallet.' You will then be redirected to the Wallet welcome screen.
8. How can I set up Face ID?
Yes, you can set up Face ID security. To do so, navigate to Security -> Face ID. After setting up your Face ID, you will be able to unlock your wallet either via Face ID or by entering your wallet passcode.
9. How do I earn tokens?
You can earn tokens in the following ways:
  • You earn 1 token for every 1000 steps you walk (max 10 tokens per day).
  • You can spin the wheel to win up to 100 Sharp daily.
  • You can refer a friend – both you and your friend will receive 100 Sharp.
  • You can also give a quiz and earn up to 10 tokens daily.
10. What happens if I walk more than 10,000 steps in a day?
- You can still walk more than 10,000 steps, but the maximum number of tokens you can earn per day is 10.
11. Do I need to open the app every day to convert my steps to tokens?
- Yes, you need to open the app at least once daily to convert your steps to tokens.
12. Can I carry over unclaimed steps from one day to the next?
- No, unclaimed steps from previous days will not be considered the next day.
13. Where can I see my steps, calories burnt, and tokens earned today?
- You can see this information on the health screen of the app.
14. How do I check my previous days' health data?
- You can find your previous days' health data under "Steps Today" on the health screen.
15. What is the difference between allocated tokens and available tokens?
- Allocated tokens are temporarily locked and will gradually become unlocked over time. Available tokens are accessible within your wallet for transactions.
16. How do I check my reward summary?
- You can check your reward summary under "Recent Transactions" on the wallet screen.
17. Where can I find my wallet address and passphrase?
- Your wallet address is under "address" in the profile screen, and your passphrase can be found in the "Security" section of the profile screen.
18. Can I change the theme of the app?
- Yes, you can choose between dark, light, and default themes from the "Settings" section of the profile screen.
19. How can I update my username and profile picture?
- You can update your username and profile picture under the profile screen of the app.
20. Can I activate/deactivate biometric authentication?
- Yes, you can activate/deactivate biometric authentication from the "Security" section of the app.
21. On what basis is the rank calculated for the leaderboard?
- User rank is calculated based on the number of tokens earned in the last day, this week, this month, and this year.
22. Can I view users’ public profiles?
- Yes, you can. Please click on the image of the desired user, and you will be redirected to their profile page. The profile displays the user’s global rank, total steps walked, and earnings made to date.
23. On what criteria is the global rank calculated?
- The global rank is determined based on the total earnings accumulated to date.
24. Can a user spin the wheel more than once?
- As of now, users are allowed only one spin/day. However, we plan to introduce a “buy more spins” option in the future.
25. What happens if I leave the app while the quiz timer is running?
- If you leave the app while the quiz timer is active, the timer will continue running in the background. When you return, you can resume the quiz. However, if the timer for all five questions has expired by the time you return, the quiz will be over.

Additionally, if the app is manually or automatically closed, you will not be given another chance to attempt the quiz.
26. How much time do I get to answer a quiz question?
- You are given 15 seconds to read and answer each question.
27. Why do I need to verify my email ID?
- Email verification is required to link your email with your wallet address. This enhances security and provides a more personalized experience.
Thank you for using the Sharp Rewards app!
